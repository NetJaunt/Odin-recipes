Ingredients Heading

  • 1 small banana
  • 1/2 cup blackberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1(6 ounce) container vanilla yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 4 ice cubes

Steps Heading

  1. Place banana, blackberries, raspberries, yogurt, honey, and ice cubes into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
